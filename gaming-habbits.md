### Category: OSINT | Difficulty: Medium | Tenant: UNbreakable

Description: Betaflash has to meet his friend at the green house from the picture. You need to find the city name, square identifiers, and part of the map in order to help him get there. EX: City_Name(x.xx:x.xx)SE

Search and you'll find that the photo is from the DayZ game. There is a specific DayZ interactive map site that will give you coordinates using squares.

If you google Dayz map railway, you can confirm it is indeed the Chernarus map. If you also search for small train stations, you will find a list (or if you want to do it like me, search for them following the railway across the entire map). Check if there is a greenhouse next to it.

Answer: Dobroye(1.29:0.03)NE
